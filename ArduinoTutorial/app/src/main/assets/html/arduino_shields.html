Arduino shields

An introduction to the Arduino Shields to extend your Arduino.


Competencies

  • Identify components in Arduino Uno's board.
  • Differentiate microcontroller's parts.
  • Learn main functionalities of microcontroller's parts.
  • Identify Arduino Uno's inputs and outputs.
