Buzzer (Predefined Musical Note)

Instruction

Description

Emits a sound with a predefined musical note (generates a frequency signal for a buzzer). You can choose the type of note, the duration of the note, and whether you want to generate a tie effect between notes.

Inputs

  • Pin: Digital pin to which the sound buzzer is connected to.
  • Note: Musical note to be generated.
  • Duration: Duration of the musical note.
  • Tie: Sets tie condition.
