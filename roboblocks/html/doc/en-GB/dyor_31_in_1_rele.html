Activates or deactivates a relay

Instruction

Description

A relay is activated or deactivated by means of the corresponding pin connected to the relay coil. In many industrial processes, a relay is associated with a power signal that must be activated or deactivated upon the case. For this reason a specific instruction has been introduced, although this instruction is identically functional to the instruction:

Inputs

  • Pin: Digital pin to read.
  • Value: HIGH to activate LOW to deactivate.

Example

In this example the current of an motor is cut off if an intruder has invaded a safety area (represented by the pushbutton).

All changes will be lost!
