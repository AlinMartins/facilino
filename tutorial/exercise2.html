Exercise 2

Identification of Arduino Multifunction Shield components

Required material. Arduino Multifunction shield, multimeter, magnifying glass (or an App).
Preliminaries

Exercise 2.1: SMD Resistors
  1. Locate SMD resistors. Locate SMD resistors in Arduino Multifunction shield. How many you can find?
  2. Resistor value. Annotate as many as resistor code you can find. Later, compute the resistance value. 
Exercise 2.2: LEDs
  1. Locate LEDs. Locate all possible LEDs you can find in the multifunctional board and indicate their type: DIP or SMD.
  2. Locate RGB LED. Locate the RGB LED and the three resistors just besides the RGB LED.
  3. LED polarity. Using the multimeter, find the correct polarity (anode and catode) of one of the DIP LEDs.
Exercise 2.3: Switches
  1. Locate switches. Locate all switches. How many of them you can find?
  2. Test switches. When pressing a switch, their contacts must be in short-circuit. Test with the multimeter that switches are working properly.
Exercise 2.4: Variable resistors
  1. Resistance measurement of the potentiometer. Locate the potentiometer and meassure its resistance. There must be at least pair of pins that the resistance value varies according the potentiometer axis position, other pins combinations might provide you with a fixed value, no matter the axis position.
  2. Resistance measurement of the LDR. Locate the LDR and measure its resistance. Does the resistance value increase when you block the light?
Exercise 2.5: Remainder of components
  1. Locate remainder of components. The DHT11 sensor is a humidity and temperature sensor, but only has three pins and the package is blue. LM35 is an analog temperature sensor. The package is black with a D-shape (top view). Buzzer generates sounds and the package is black with a rounded shape (top view). The IR receiver is also black with three pins.
  2. Locate General purpose pins. The multifunctional shield has free pins available to the user for general purpose. They are accessible through male pin header connectors. Locate all available pins for general purpose.
