Exercise 5.1: Logic Operations
  1. AND and OR Operations.. Implement the AND operation using the button switches (connected to pins D2 and D3) and the red and blue LEDs (connected to pin D12 and D13). The aim is to implement the following logic operations: RED=SW1∧SW2 (AND Operation), BLUE=SW1∨SW2 (OR operation).
  2. Binary negation.. Boole algrebra establishes that RED=SW1∧SW2=(¬SW1)∨(¬SW2), as well as BLUE=SW1∨SW2=¬(SW1∧SW2). Check it!
Exercise 5.2: Logic Comparison
  1. Segmenting potentiometer values. The potentiometer connected to pin A0 can provide values between 0 and 1023. However, we are interested in discriminating four diferent range of values: range1=[0,255], range2=[256,511], range3=[512,767] and range4=[768,1023]. Indicate each of the ranges by turning on and off the red and blue LEDs (connected to pins D12 and D13) so that red and blue are off in range1; red is on and blue is off in range2; red is off and blue is on in range3; and red and blue are on in range4.
