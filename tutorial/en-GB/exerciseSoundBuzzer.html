Sound Buzzer

Learn how to generate predefined sounds, melodies and even melodies that run in the background meanwhile you can do other stuff.

Required material.
  • Arduino Uno (and compatible) or Wemos D1R2/D1R32.
  • Arduino multisensor shield.
